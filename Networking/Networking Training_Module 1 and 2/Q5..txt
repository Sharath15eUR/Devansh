5. Understand what happens when duplicate IPs configured in a network.


Answer: When two devices have the same IP:
		1.Network conflicts occur, causing intermittent connection loss.
		2.ARP table inconsistencies arise.
		3.We can use arp -a to check conflicting IPs.
		4.Resolve by assigning static IPs or enabling DHCP.
